Recently I did some work on integrating TogetherJS into Popcorn Maker and it has now landed. For anyone who hasn't heard of TogetherJS yet it's a tool created by Mozilla that allows easy collaboration on websites.

If you want to collaborate on Popcorn Maker now all you need to do is:

  • Head over to popcorn.webmaker.org
  • Sign in at the top of the page.
  • Hover over your name to get the drop down.
  • Click collaborate!
Share the link that you get from the TogetherJS side bar and you'll be able to to collaborate with your friends in real time, hassle free.

Thanks to everyone who helped me out with getting this integrated. Special thanks to Ian Bicking whose a primary developer on TogetherJS and took a lot of time out of his day to help me.

That's not it for TogetherJS and Popcorn Maker though! There's still lots of improvements and features coming, so look out for more TogetherJS and Popcorn Maker in the future!
